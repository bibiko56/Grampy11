Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) GRAMPY Clothing

Welcome to the GRAMPY Family! We're thrilled you're interested in our Iloilo-made apparel. Here are some answers to common questions you might have:


Ordering & Shipping


Q: Where can I purchase GRAMPY clothing?

A: You can purchase our products exclusively through our official website and select pop-up events within Iloilo City and occasionally in neighboring regions. Follow us on our social media accounts for announcements about these events!


Q: Do you offer international shipping?

A: Currently, we primarily ship within the Philippines. However, we are exploring international shipping options for the future. Please stay tuned for updates!


Q: How long does shipping take within the Philippines?

A: Shipping times vary depending on your location. Generally, orders within Iloilo City are delivered within 2-3 business days, while orders outside Iloilo may take 3-7 business days. Please allow for potential delays during peak seasons or due to unforeseen circumstances.


Q: What are your shipping fees?

A: Shipping fees are calculated based on your location and the weight of your order. You can view the shipping costs at checkout before completing your purchase.


Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, GCash, and bank transfers.



Products & Care


Q: What materials are GRAMPY clothing made from?

A: We prioritize quality and comfort. Our clothing is made from a variety of premium materials, including cotton blends, rayon, etc. Please refer to the product description for specific material details.


Q: How do I care for my GRAMPY clothing?

A: To ensure the longevity of your GRAMPY apparel, we recommend machine washing in cold water with similar colors and tumble drying on low. Avoid using bleach and iron on low heat if necessary. Specific care instructions are also provided on the garment's label.


Q: Do you offer different sizes?

A: Yes, we offer a range of sizes from Small to Extra Large. Please refer to our size chart on each product page for accurate measurements.


Q: Are your designs original?

A: Absolutely! All our designs are original creations by our talented team of Ilonggo artists and designers. We take pride in showcasing unique and locally inspired artwork.



Returns & Exchanges


Q: What is your return/exchange policy?

A: We accept returns and exchanges for items that are unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition within 4 days of delivery. Please refer to our detailed Return/Exchange Policy on our website for more information.


Q: How do I initiate a return or exchange?

A: To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer support team at emn.msqda0502@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return/exchange. We will guide you through the process.
